Tanh (Hyperbolic Tangent) activation function Tanh（双曲線正接）活性化関数

Applies the element-wise function: Tanh(x) = (exp(x) - exp(-x)) / (exp(x) + exp(-x)) 要素ごとに関数を適用: Tanh(x) = (exp(x) - exp(-x)) / (exp(x) + exp(-x))
